Otters

The best animals ever

I love otters - Valeria Martinez, 2020

Otters are charismatic mammals.

They are members of the weasel family.

    Here are some fun facts about otters:

  • Otters can measure up to 4ft and weigh up to 70lbs
  • Otters can swim 14kph. They are super fast!
  • Otters hold hands while sleeping to prevent losing each other.
